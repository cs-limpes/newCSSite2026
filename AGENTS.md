# AGENTS.md

This site is CharleneSlimp.com, a static professional-intellectual website.

Priorities:
- Plain HTML/CSS/JS only
- No frameworks, CMS, package managers, or build steps unless explicitly requested
- Maintain semantic, accessible HTML
- Keep pages fast, lightweight, and easy to edit manually
- Preserve an academically adjacent, IndieWeb-inspired tone
- Avoid sales funnel language, marketing hype, popups, newsletter CTAs, or aggressive conversion patterns
- Do not expose a plain email address
- Use restrained typography, whitespace, and simple navigation
- Prioritize research, selected work, current focus, CV, and contact

Voice:
Clear, grounded, thoughtful, interdisciplinary, not overly corporate, not spiritual/woo-focused.

Do not add:
- Calendly links
- newsletter signup
- analytics scripts
- tracking pixels
- heavy animations
- stock photos
- AI-generated buzzword copy
